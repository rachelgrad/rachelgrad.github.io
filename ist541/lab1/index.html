How To Spot A Scam

By Rachel Grad


Step 1: Look At The Sender Address

Look closely at the second part of the sender's email address, after the @. It may be misspelled or even nonsense. Legitimate organizations will never send from common email providers like @gmail.com (not even Google!). It may include convincing text like "payhelp,"" a generic human name, or even a real company logo, but don't be fooled.
